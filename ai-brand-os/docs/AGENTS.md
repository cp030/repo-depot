# AI Agents

## Agent Pipeline

1. **Intake** — Collects and structures client brief data
2. **Audit** — Reviews existing brand assets
3. **Research** — Gathers market and audience insights
4. **Competitive** — Analyzes competitor positioning
5. **Strategy** — Synthesizes findings into brand strategy
6. **Production** — Generates brand deliverables
7. **QA** — Validates outputs against brief
8. **Consistency** — Enforces brand consistency across all outputs
